== Description ==

	Inventive stock player is a premium wordpress plugin created for videomakers and musicians whose have their videos and audioprojects on different music libraries.

We musicians know that a lot of time we need our music works in a lot of music libraries, one because it is good to have our music shared in a lot of places and also because sometimes our work get rejected and we have one song in one place and one in another.

With this plugin, pasting just the link of you audio/video in your wordpress content from libraries like audiojungle, pond5, videohive, luckaudio, soundcloud, you will have an unique audio player which streams from these stock websites, so your portfolio on your site will be more homogenous with native wordpress audioplayer. 



**MAIN FEATURES OF THE LITE VERSION**

1. widget ready for audiojungle and soundcloud

2. Just paste your link (ex. http://www.pond5.com/stock-music/50384963/traveling-secrets.html) it will automatically retrieve mp3 file and play it on wordpress default player.

3. Cool Spectrum analyzer for your audio, you can place it in 5 different places and choose colors and spectrum bars number,


**MAIN FEATURES OF THE PRO VERSION**

1. Widget ready for audiojungle, soundcloud, pond5 audio, pond5 video, videohive, luckstock,

1. Just paste your link (ex. http://www.pond5.com/stock-music/50384963/traveling-secrets.html) it will automatically retrieve mp3 file and play it on wordpress default player,

2. Cool Spectrum analyzer for your audio, you can place it in 5 different places and choose colors and spectrum bars number,

3. You can place player in substitution of featured image, above and under title, above and under description,

4. You can show player in products and post listings,

5. You can put automatically a buy button with referral in list and single post/pages/products,

6. Compatible with inventive 3d world,

7. Woocommerce ready,

8. Multilingual ready,

9. Widget included,

10.Shortcode included,
